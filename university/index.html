Courses We Offer

Whether you are looking for post-baccalaureate certificates, online Master's programs, residential Master's or Doctoral programs, SIUC has plenty of available options.

Intermediate

Our Mechanical Engineering faculty come to SIU from major universities across the nation. They will be your teachers, your mentors, and your research guides.


They put their own research in your classroom, giving you practical experience and access to equipment you won’t find just anywhere – labs for thermal management, biomechanics, non-destructive evaluation, tracking by micro-robotics, materials processing for fuel, cells and more.

About Us

SIUE is a nationally recognized university that provides students with a high-quality education that powerfully transforms the lives of all individuals who seek something greater. A premier metropolitan university, SIUE is creating social and economic mobility for individuals while also powering the workforce of the future. Built on the foundation of a broad-based liberal education, and enhanced by hands-on research and real-world experiences, the academic preparation SIUE students receive equips them to thrive in the global marketplace and make our communities better places to live.
